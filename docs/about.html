What is NetGuard? ​

NetGuard is a completely free solution for protecting against DDoS attacks. You can easily build your own defense system by integrating our SDK into your app and enjoy robust protection.

Why Use NetGuard? ​

The Threat of DDoS ​

With advancing technology, the cost of launching a DDoS (Denial of Service) attack has decreased, leaving developers constantly at risk of such attacks on their servers. DDoS attacks can render services unavailable, cause network congestion, and increase latency, placing immense pressure on applications and systems.

Developers often spend significant time and resources mitigating attacks and protecting their servers and infrastructure, which diverts focus from development and delays project timelines. Moreover, DDoS attacks can lead to data breaches and theft of user information, compromising privacy and data security. Developers need reliable protection to ensure application stability and security. Click here for more information on DDoS attacks.

Flaws in Other Solutions ​

  1. Weak Defense: Most servers only handle limited DDoS attacks.
  2. High Cost: Many solutions start at thousands, even tens of thousands.
  3. Poor Support: Many providers lack R&D, struggling with strong attacks.
  4. Complex Tech: Most developers can’t build their own DDoS defense.

Advantages of NetGuard ​

  1. Forever Free: Self-host servers and Build Your Own Defence System.
  2. Shared Defense: Or Join the Shared Defense Plan to reduce costs.
  3. Easy Integration: Powerful protection with simple SDK integration.
  4. Auto Reconnect: SDK automatically switches to other servers if disconnected.
  5. No Downtime: Real-Time Data Sync Between Nodes for Seamless Switching.
